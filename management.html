Getting Started

How Onboarding Works

From first conversation to fully live — here's exactly what happens and when.

1
Day 1

Free Revenue Analysis Call

We start with a no-obligation call to review your property, discuss your goals, and show you what comparable properties in your market are earning. You'll leave knowing exactly what your property should be making.

2
Day 1–2

Onboarding Form Sent

We send you a detailed onboarding form covering everything we need: property specs, bedroom/bathroom layout, amenities, access instructions, house rules, preferred guest types, and any existing listing details. The more we know, the better we perform.

3
Day 2–3

Onboarding Strategy Call

Once we review your form, we hop on a strategy call to lock in your pricing approach, set up automated check-in and check-out messages, build your guest communication sequence, and align on your minimum stay rules and seasonal strategy.

4
Day 3–5

Listing Build-Out

We craft SEO-optimized listings with titles and descriptions designed to rank in Airbnb and VRBO search. Every amenity is documented, every selling point highlighted. We also advise on photography if needed — great photos are the #1 conversion driver.

5
Day 5–7

Channel Manager Activation

Your property goes live across 15+ booking platforms simultaneously — Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, Expedia, Hopper, and more. Our channel manager keeps all calendars in real-time sync so double bookings are impossible.

6
Week 2

Direct Booking Website Created

We build you a standalone direct booking website for your property. This lets repeat guests and referrals book directly — bypassing OTA service fees and putting more money in your pocket on every reservation.

7
Week 2

Guest Portal Setup

Guests get access to a self-serve portal where they can request early check-in, late checkout, and add-on services. These ancillary revenue streams — even at $50–100 per request — add up significantly over the course of a year.

8
Ongoing

Continuous Optimization

Rates are reviewed and adjusted daily. We monitor market demand, local events, competitor pricing, and calendar gaps. You receive monthly performance reports benchmarking your revenue, occupancy, and ADR against comparable properties.

What We Do

Our Full Management Platform

Every tool, strategy, and system we use to maximize your property's performance.

Channel Management

We list and manage your property across 15+ booking platforms simultaneously. A channel manager keeps all calendars in real-time sync — zero double bookings, maximum exposure.

  • Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, Expedia
  • Hopper, Tripadvisor & more
  • Real-time calendar sync across all channels

Dynamic Pricing Engine

Rates are reviewed and adjusted based on market demand, local events, seasonality, competitor pricing, and days-until-arrival. The right rate at the right time fills more nights at higher prices.

  • Daily rate adjustments
  • Event & holiday pricing spikes
  • Last-minute & advance booking strategy

Direct Booking Website

OTA platforms charge guests 12–15% service fees, which reduces your competitiveness. We build you a professional direct booking site so guests can book without OTA fees — increasing your net revenue per booking.

  • Your own branded booking page
  • Eliminates OTA service fees for guests
  • Captures repeat & referral bookings

Guest Portal & Ancillary Revenue

Our guest portal lets guests self-serve early check-in, late checkout, and add-on requests. These generate extra revenue beyond the nightly rate that adds up fast over the course of a year.

  • Early check-in / late checkout fees
  • Add-on service requests
  • Automated approval & payment

Automated Guest Messaging

Every booking triggers a professional communication sequence — confirmation, pre-arrival instructions, door codes, local recommendations, mid-stay check-in, and a post-stay review request.

  • Instant booking confirmations
  • Automated check-in instructions
  • Review request sequences

Listing SEO Optimization

We write titles and descriptions engineered to rank in Airbnb and VRBO search. Strategic use of amenities, keywords, and photo order to maximize click-through rate and conversion.

  • Keyword-optimized titles & descriptions
  • Amenity & photo sequencing
  • Ongoing A/B testing & updates

OTA Promotion Management

Airbnb and VRBO offer promotional tools that boost algorithm visibility. We strategically activate early bird discounts, last-minute deals, and weekly rates to fill calendar gaps without leaving money on the table.

  • Early bird & last-minute discounts
  • Weekly & monthly rate management
  • Algorithm visibility optimization

Revenue & Performance Reporting

Monthly reports showing your revenue, occupancy rate, ADR (average daily rate), and market comparison data. You always know exactly how your property is performing relative to the market.

  • Monthly performance summaries
  • Market benchmark comparisons
  • Occupancy & ADR tracking

Pricing Strategy by Property Type

A 2BR condo and a 5BR pool home require completely different strategies. We build custom pricing profiles based on your property's size, amenities, location, and the specific demand patterns in your market.

  • Size & amenity-based pricing
  • Seasonal demand curves
  • Minimum stay optimization
