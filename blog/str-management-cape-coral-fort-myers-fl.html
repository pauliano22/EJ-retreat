The Cape Coral / Fort Myers Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Cape Coral and Fort Myers offer a compelling combination for STR investors: a warm climate that attracts visitors nearly every month of the year, an expanding network of canals and Gulf-access properties, and a tourism infrastructure that keeps demand high. Markets like Cape Coral / Fort Myers often see strong performance from waterfront homes, pool villas, and properties close to popular destinations such as Fort Myers Beach, Sanibel Island, and downtown Fort Myers' arts and dining district.

Demand drivers in the area include:

  • Snowbird season (November through April) bringing extended-stay guests from the Northeast and Midwest
  • Spring training baseball tourism tied to the nearby JetBlue Park and Hammond Stadium
  • Gulf beach access and boating culture drawing summer and holiday travelers
  • A steady stream of corporate and relocation travelers given the region's rapid population growth

Many operators report average daily rates (ADR) ranging from the mid-$100s to well over $300 for premium waterfront listings, with peak-season occupancy frequently exceeding 80 percent for well-positioned properties.
