Park City's Short-Term Rental Market: A Strong Opportunity for Property Owners

Park City consistently ranks among Utah's top destinations for leisure travel, anchored by Park City Mountain Resort and Deer Valley — two resorts that together draw millions of skiers and snowboarders each winter. Beyond ski season, the Sundance Film Festival, mountain biking trails, and a thriving arts scene keep demand elevated from January through December.

Popular neighborhoods for STR investors include Old Town (walkable to Main Street and ski lifts), Deer Valley's upper corridors, Jeremy Ranch, and Kimball Junction near the outlet shops and Highway 80. Many operators in markets like Park City report occupancy rates well above national averages during peak winter and summer windows.

  • Proximity to two major ski resorts drives strong winter demand
  • Sundance Film Festival creates an intense annual demand spike each January
  • Summer outdoor recreation extends the high season well beyond ski months
  • High household-income visitor base supports above-average nightly rates
