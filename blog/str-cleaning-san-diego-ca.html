San Diego STR Cleaning Standards: What Hosts Need to Know

Vacation rental turnover cleaning in San Diego is a fundamentally different discipline from standard residential cleaning. Guests arrive with high expectations set by hotel-quality listings, and they notice everything — sand tracked into grout lines after a beach stay, a single hair on a pillow, or a sticky residue on the kitchen counter. Many operators report that cleanliness is the number-one driver of their overall review score on platforms like Airbnb and Vrbo.

San Diego properties also face unique physical demands. Coastal homes near Mission Beach or La Jolla deal with salt air, sand, and moisture that accelerate wear and require more thorough wipe-downs of surfaces, window tracks, and sliding doors. Inland properties near Balboa Park or North Park often see higher guest turnover volume during event weekends, compressing check-out and check-in windows to just a few hours.

  • Incomplete turnovers lead directly to negative reviews and potential platform penalties
