The Hilton Head Island Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Hilton Head Island attracts a diverse mix of leisure travelers, golf enthusiasts, and family vacationers throughout the year, creating consistent short-term rental demand that many coastal markets envy. The island's 12 miles of pristine beach, more than 24 golf courses, and protected Sea Pines Resort draw guests who are willing to pay a premium for well-appointed accommodations.

Popular neighborhoods for STR investors include Sea Pines, Palmetto Dunes, Shipyard, and Forest Beach — each offering proximity to amenities that guests specifically seek out. Markets like Hilton Head Island often see average daily rates ranging from the mid-$200s to well above $500 for larger homes during peak periods, with occupancy rates that many operators report exceeding 65–75% annually when properties are actively managed.

  • Year-round demand anchored by golf season (spring and fall) and beach season (summer)
  • Strong family and group travel drives demand for multi-bedroom homes
  • Proximity to Savannah, Atlanta, and Charlotte expands the drive-to-market audience
  • Gated resort communities command premium nightly rates
