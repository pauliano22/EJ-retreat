The Naples Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Naples consistently ranks among Florida's top-performing short-term rental destinations. The city attracts a high-net-worth traveler demographic drawn to its pristine Gulf beaches, boutique shopping on Fifth Avenue South, and more than 90 golf courses within a short drive. This translates into strong demand for well-appointed vacation rentals that can command premium nightly rates.

Popular neighborhoods for STR investors in Naples include Old Naples, Vanderbilt Beach, Pelican Bay, and the Moorings — areas where proximity to the water and walkability to dining drive occupancy. Many operators in markets like Naples report average daily rates (ADR) that significantly exceed the Florida statewide average, particularly in winter and spring.

  • Peak season typically runs November through April, when Northern snowbirds and holiday travelers arrive
  • Summer shoulder season benefits from domestic family travel and beach tourism
  • Luxury and waterfront properties in Naples often see ADRs well above $300 per night
  • High-quality listings with professional photos and dynamic pricing typically achieve occupancy rates of 65–80%
