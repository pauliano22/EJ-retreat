The Steamboat Springs Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Steamboat Springs sits at the heart of Routt County and benefits from a dual-season tourism economy that few Colorado mountain towns can match. Ski season at Steamboat Resort drives strong winter bookings from November through early April, while summer demand — fueled by mountain biking, hiking, fishing, and the famous Strawberry Park Hot Springs — keeps occupancy healthy well beyond the snow months.

Popular areas for short-term rentals include the Ski Time Square corridor, Steamboat's Old Town district, and neighborhoods within a short drive of the gondola base area. Properties in these pockets often command premium nightly rates due to walkability and proximity to resort amenities.

  • Year-round demand driven by skiing, hiking, hot springs, and festivals
  • Strong weekend and holiday compression pricing opportunities
  • Growing interest from remote workers extending stays mid-week
  • Markets like Steamboat Springs typically see above-average ADR compared to non-resort Colorado towns
