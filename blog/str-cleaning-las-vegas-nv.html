STR Turnover Cleaning Challenges and Standards in Las Vegas

Las Vegas short-term rentals operate at a tempo that leaves little room for error. Many operators report same-day turnovers during peak periods — a guest checks out in the morning and a new one arrives that afternoon. In that tight window, every surface, linen, and amenity must be guest-ready to the standard travelers have come to expect from hotel-quality stays.

STR turnover cleaning goes far beyond a standard residential clean. It involves a full reset of the property: stripping and replacing all bedding, sanitizing high-touch surfaces, restocking toiletries and supplies, inspecting for damage, and staging the space so it photographs well for the next booking. Markets like Las Vegas often see guests who entertain, host groups, or keep unusual hours — all factors that increase the likelihood of a harder-than-average turnover.

  • Missed spots, stained linens, or low supplies result in negative reviews that are difficult to recover from
  • A single poor-cleanliness review can drop a listing's search ranking and cut into nightly revenue
  • Inconsistent cleaning creates unpredictable guest experiences that undermine repeat bookings
