STR Turnover Cleaning Challenges and Standards in Cape Coral / Fort Myers

The Cape Coral / Fort Myers market presents a unique set of cleaning demands. Canal-front and Gulf-access properties attract guests who arrive with boats, fishing gear, sandy swimwear, and saltwater residue that works its way into grout, upholstery, and flooring. High-season occupancy rates mean same-day turnovers are common, giving cleaners a narrow window — often just two to four hours — to fully reset a property between checkout and the next check-in.

STR turnover cleaning is fundamentally different from routine residential cleaning. Every surface must meet guest-ready standards on every visit, not just periodically. That means:

  • Stripping, laundering, and restaging all linens and towels
  • Restocking consumables (toiletries, coffee, paper goods)
  • Inspecting for damage and flagging maintenance issues
  • Sanitizing high-touch points between every guest stay
  • Resetting the property to a consistent, photo-ready condition

Many operators in markets like Cape Coral / Fort Myers report that cleanliness is the single most cited factor in five-star reviews — and the fastest way to lose them. Unreliable cleaning partners who no-show or cut corners force hosts to scramble, accept negative reviews, or lose bookings entirely.
