STR Turnover Cleaning Challenges and Standards on Hilton Head Island

Hilton Head Island's STR market moves fast. During summer and holiday weekends, many properties cycle through check-out and check-in on the same day, sometimes within a three- to four-hour window. That pace leaves no room for inconsistency — sand tracked in from the beach, sunscreen residue on furniture, and heavy use of outdoor amenities are routine challenges that require a cleaning team trained specifically for vacation rental properties, not standard residential work.

STR turnover cleaning differs from routine housekeeping in scope and accountability. Every turn must cover:

  • Full linen strip and replacement across all bedrooms and bathrooms
  • Kitchen reset including appliance interiors, counters, and dish checks
  • Outdoor spaces, entry areas, and high-touch surface disinfection
  • Restocking of guest supplies and amenity staging

Markets like Hilton Head Island often see a direct correlation between cleaning reliability and review scores. Many operators report that a single substandard turn — missed hair in the shower, a sandy floor, or low toilet paper — can produce a three-star review that suppresses bookings for weeks. The stakes make professional, system-driven cleaning essential.
