The Tybee Island Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Tybee Island sits just 18 miles east of Savannah, positioning it perfectly as both a beach escape and a spillover destination when Savannah hotels fill during major events. Demand drivers include summer family vacations, bachelorette and wedding parties, Savannah St. Patrick's Day celebrations, and shoulder-season retreats for couples and remote workers drawn to the island's laid-back character.

Popular pockets for short-term rentals include the North Beach area near the historic lighthouse, mid-island streets within walking distance of local restaurants, and oceanfront or ocean-view properties along Butler Avenue. Well-located homes and cottages in these areas typically attract strong occupancy, and many operators report average daily rates that outperform comparable inland markets, particularly during peak summer weeks and holiday weekends.

  • Strong summer and holiday weekend demand from Atlanta, Charlotte, and regional drive markets
  • Year-round appeal tied to Savannah's event calendar
  • High guest appetite for entire-home rentals with beach gear, outdoor space, and pet-friendly policies
  • Limited new hotel supply on the island keeps STR competition in check
