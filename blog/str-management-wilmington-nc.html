Wilmington's Short-Term Rental Market: A Strong Opportunity for Property Owners

Wilmington sits at the intersection of coastal charm and urban energy, making it a perennial favorite for leisure travelers, remote workers, and event-driven visitors alike. Demand drivers include Wrightsville Beach, Carolina Beach, the historic riverfront, and a packed calendar of festivals, film productions, and university events tied to UNC Wilmington.

Popular neighborhoods for short-term rentals span a wide range of guest preferences:

  • Historic Downtown Wilmington — walkable, restaurant-rich, and ideal for weekend getaways
  • Wrightsville Beach area — peak summer demand with strong oceanfront premiums
  • Midtown and Ogden — family-friendly locations with easy access to both beach and city
  • Carolina Beach / Kure Beach — laid-back coastal vibe with loyal repeat visitors

Many operators in markets like Wilmington report occupancy rates ranging from 60–80% annually for well-positioned listings, with average daily rates that typically climb during summer months and holiday weekends. This mix of consistent shoulder-season travel and high-peak summer demand makes Wilmington an attractive market for STR investors seeking year-round cash flow.
