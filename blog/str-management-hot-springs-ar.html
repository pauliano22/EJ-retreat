The Hot Springs Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Hot Springs sits at the intersection of natural beauty and cultural history, drawing a diverse mix of guests year-round. The national park, thermal bathhouses, lakeside recreation on Lake Hamilton and Lake Ouachita, and a growing arts and dining scene give property owners multiple demand engines rather than reliance on a single tourism driver.

Neighborhoods and property types that tend to perform well for STR investors include:

  • Lakefront and lake-view homes on Lake Hamilton, which command premium nightly rates
  • Cabins and cottages within a short drive of Garvan Woodland Gardens and Ouachita National Forest
  • Downtown and mid-town properties convenient to Bathhouse Row and Central Avenue dining
  • Larger group-friendly homes catering to family reunions and corporate retreats

Markets like Hot Springs often see average daily rates ranging from $150 to over $300 for well-appointed properties, with occupancy rates that many operators report in the 60–80% range during strong seasons. The year-round appeal — holidays, spring wildflower season, summer lake activity, and fall foliage — makes Hot Springs a compelling destination for STR investment.

Revenue Potential for Short-Term Rentals in Hot Springs

The revenue upside for a well-managed short-term rental in Hot Springs, Arkansas can be substantial when compared to traditional long-term leasing. Many operators report annual gross revenues in the range of $30,000 to $80,000 or more for mid-size properties, with lakefront homes and larger group accommodations often exceeding those figures during strong years.

Several factors drive higher ADR and occupancy in the Hot Springs market:

  • Peak summer demand from lake recreation and family travel (June–August)
  • Fall color season and Garvan Gardens events driving strong September–November bookings
  • Holiday weekends year-round, particularly Memorial Day, Labor Day, and New Year's
  • Wedding and event groups seeking large private homes for multi-night stays

Self-managed properties often leave revenue on the table through static pricing, limited platform exposure, and delayed guest responses. Professional STR management in Hot Springs through E&J Retreats typically helps owners capture higher average rates during demand peaks while maintaining competitive pricing to fill shoulder-season gaps — resulting in meaningfully better annual returns than most owners achieve on their own.
