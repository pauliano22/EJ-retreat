STR Turnover Cleaning Challenges and Standards in Myrtle Beach

Myrtle Beach vacation rentals face a level of guest volume that makes turnover cleaning uniquely demanding. During summer weekends and holiday weeks, many properties flip multiple times within 24 hours, leaving cleaning teams only a narrow window — often two to four hours — to fully reset a unit to hotel-level standards. Markets like Myrtle Beach often see occupancy patterns that make same-day double turnovers the norm rather than the exception.

STR turnover cleaning goes far beyond what a standard housekeeping visit covers. Every clean must address:

  • Full linen and towel changeouts to fresh, laundered sets
  • Deep sanitization of bathrooms and kitchen surfaces
  • Restocking consumables such as toiletries, paper goods, and coffee supplies
  • Inspection and reporting of damage or missing items before the next guest arrives
  • Staging of the space to match listing photos

Many operators report that a single subpar clean resulting in a negative guest review can suppress booking momentum for weeks. In a competitive coastal market, unreliable cleaning is one of the fastest routes to lower star ratings, increased refund requests, and eventual delisting from major platforms.
