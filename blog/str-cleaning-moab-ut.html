STR turnover cleaning is fundamentally different from routine housekeeping — each clean must reset the property to a hotel-quality baseline, not simply tidy what guests disturbed. Many operators in markets like Moab report that cleanliness is the single most-cited factor in both five-star reviews and one-star complaints. A property that looks great in listing photos but arrives dirty will lose guests permanently. Common failure points include:
