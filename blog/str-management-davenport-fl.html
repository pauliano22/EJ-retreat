Do I need a license or permit to operate a short-term rental in Davenport, FL?

Yes — short-term rental operators in Davenport and Polk County are generally required to obtain a Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation (DBPR) vacation rental license as well as a local business tax receipt, and must collect and remit applicable state and local tourist development taxes. Regulations can change, so it is important to verify current requirements with the City of Davenport and Polk County before listing your property.
