The St. Augustine Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

St. Augustine consistently ranks among Florida's top leisure destinations, fueled by its status as the oldest city in the United States, its walkable historic district, and proximity to Atlantic beaches. This combination generates demand from history travelers, weekend getaway visitors, beach vacationers, and event attendees throughout the calendar year.

Popular neighborhoods and areas for short-term rentals in St. Augustine include:

  • The Historic District and St. George Street corridor — high foot-traffic, premium nightly rates
  • Vilano Beach and Anastasia Island — beach-access properties with strong seasonal demand
  • Uptown and Lincolnville — charming character homes with growing investor interest
  • St. Augustine Beach — family-friendly market with reliable occupancy across summer months

Markets like St. Augustine often see occupancy rates that outperform state averages during peak seasons, with many operators reporting average daily rates (ADR) that reflect the city's premium positioning as a cultural and coastal destination. For STR investors, this translates into a compelling income opportunity when properties are managed effectively.
