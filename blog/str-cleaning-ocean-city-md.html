Ocean City's peak season compresses an enormous number of turnovers into a narrow band of weeks. Many vacation rental operators report same-day flips where the outgoing guest checks out at 10 a.m. and an incoming family arrives by 4 p.m. — leaving a four- to six-hour window to clean, inspect, restock, and photograph an entire property. That pace is nothing like a routine residential clean; it requires choreography, trained staff, and reliable backup coverage when a cleaner calls out.

STR turnover cleaning in Ocean City also means meeting guests who have compared your property to dozens of listings online. They arrive expecting hotel-level presentation: streak-free mirrors, sand-free floors, spotless kitchen appliances, and fresh linens that smell clean rather than musty from storage. Markets like Ocean City often see a direct correlation between cleaning scores and overall listing rank, meaning a string of 3-star cleanliness reviews can suppress your visibility for an entire season.
