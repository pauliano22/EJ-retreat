Denver STR Cleaning Standards: Why Vacation Rental Turnovers Demand More

Short-term rental turnover cleaning is a fundamentally different discipline from standard residential housekeeping. In a market like Denver, where guests arrive with high expectations shaped by hotel-level platforms like Airbnb and Vrbo, the bar for cleanliness is unforgiving. A single overlooked detail — a hair on a pillow, a sticky countertop, a missed restocking of supplies — can translate directly into a negative review.

Denver properties also face unique pressure from the pace of bookings. Many operators report back-to-back turnovers with windows of just two to four hours between checkout and the next check-in. That timeline demands a team that is organized, accountable, and familiar with the specific layout and standards of each property.

  • Full kitchen sanitation including appliances, surfaces, and dishware
  • Bathroom deep-clean and restocking of guest amenities
  • Linen swap, bed-making to hotel standard, and staging of common areas
  • Trash removal, recycling, and exterior tidiness checks
  • Damage inspection and photo documentation after every clean
