Do I need a permit or license to operate a short-term rental in San Antonio?

San Antonio has STR regulations that require property owners to register with the city and collect Hotel Occupancy Tax (HOT) on guest stays. Requirements can vary based on property type and zoning, so it is important to verify current rules with the City of San Antonio's Development Services Department before listing.
