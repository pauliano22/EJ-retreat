Charleston STR Cleaning Standards: Why Vacation Rental Turnovers Are Different

Short-term rental cleaning in Charleston operates on a different level than routine housekeeping. In a market like Charleston, where many properties compete for the same travel weekends — think Spoleto Festival, SEWE, and spring and fall tourist peaks — a negative review can cost you weeks of future bookings.

Turnovers in Charleston also move fast. Many operators report same-day checkout and check-in windows of four hours or fewer, particularly during peak season. That pace demands cleaners who know the property, follow a consistent checklist, and do not need supervision. Standard cleaning services are rarely equipped for this:

  • No STR-specific checklist or room-by-room SOPs
