Atlanta STR Cleaning Standards: What the Market Demands

Atlanta's STR market draws business travelers, families visiting attractions like the Georgia Aquarium, and guests attending events at State Farm Arena and Mercedes-Benz Stadium. That diversity means guest expectations shift rapidly between bookings, and turnover windows are often tight — many operators in markets like Atlanta report same-day checkouts followed by same-day check-ins, leaving as little as three to four hours to reset an entire property.

STR turnover cleaning goes well beyond routine housekeeping. It includes a full linen strip and replacement, restocking of consumables, appliance wipe-downs, odor elimination, and a guest-ready inspection — every single visit, not just periodically.
