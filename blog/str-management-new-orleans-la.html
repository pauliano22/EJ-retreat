The New Orleans Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

New Orleans attracts a uniquely diverse mix of leisure travelers, convention attendees, and destination wedding guests, making it one of the most resilient STR markets in the South. Neighborhoods like the French Quarter, Marigny, Bywater, Garden District, and Mid-City all draw strong interest from travelers seeking an authentic local experience rather than a standard hotel stay.

Many operators in markets like New Orleans report average daily rates (ADR) that climb well above national averages during major events, with occupancy often tracking between 65% and 80% for well-positioned listings. The city's calendar of recurring festivals and conventions provides a predictable cadence of high-demand windows that savvy STR owners can plan around.

  • Major demand drivers: Mardi Gras, Jazz Fest, French Quarter Festival, Essence Fest, and Sugar Bowl
  • Popular STR neighborhoods: French Quarter, Marigny, Bywater, Garden District, Uptown, Mid-City
  • Strong mix of leisure, group, and business travel sustains year-round demand
  • Travelers in New Orleans typically prioritize character, location, and local flavor over standard amenities
