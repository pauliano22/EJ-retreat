The Anaheim Short-Term Rental Market Opportunity

Anaheim benefits from a rare combination of year-round leisure demand and steady corporate and convention traffic. Disneyland Resort alone draws more than 18 million visitors annually, and events at the Anaheim Convention Center and Honda Center fill calendars across every season. That diversified demand base gives Anaheim short-term rentals a resilience that purely seasonal markets often lack.

Neighborhoods close to the Resort District — including areas around Ball Road, Katella Avenue, and West Anaheim — tend to perform especially well, while properties in quieter residential pockets attract families seeking more space than a hotel room offers. Many operators report occupancy rates well above the national STR average in peak periods such as summer and the winter holiday season.
