STR Turnover Cleaning Standards in St. George: Why the Bar Is Higher Than You Think

St. George STR properties face a pace of turnover that regular residential cleaning companies simply aren't built to handle. Back-to-back bookings, same-day checkouts and check-ins, and guests arriving after long drives from Las Vegas or Salt Lake City all create pressure to deliver a hotel-quality clean on a tight clock. Many operators in markets like St. George report that even a single missed clean — or a clean that falls short — can trigger a negative review that suppresses their listing for weeks.

STR turnover cleaning goes well beyond tidying up. A professional turnover includes:

  • Full linen strip, wash, and hotel-fold restaging of all beds
  • Deep cleaning of bathrooms, including grout, fixtures, and behind toilets
  • Kitchen sanitization including appliance interiors and cabinet fronts
  • Restocking of consumables (soap, paper products, coffee supplies)
  • Damage and missing-item inspection with photo documentation
  • Outdoor area reset — patios, BBQ grills, and entry spaces

In St. George's competitive rental environment, guests compare their experience directly against nearby properties. Inconsistent cleaning is typically the top driver of sub-5-star reviews, and even one or two can push a listing down in Airbnb or Vrbo search rankings at the peak of booking season.
