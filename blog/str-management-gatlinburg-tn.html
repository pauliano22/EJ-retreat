Gatlinburg draws visitors throughout every season — spring wildflower blooms, summer family vacations, fall foliage, and winter ski trips to nearby Ober Mountain keep occupancy elevated in ways that purely seasonal beach or lake markets simply cannot match. Markets like Gatlinburg often see average annual occupancy rates well above 60%, with peak periods pushing toward 85–90% for well-positioned properties.

Proximity to Dollywood, the Parkway entertainment district, and national park trailheads are consistently cited by guests as top booking drivers.
