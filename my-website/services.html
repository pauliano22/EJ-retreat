Frequently Asked Questions

How are we different from other property management companies?

Unlike our competitors, we personally visit every property we manage to understand its unique characteristics and market positioning. We provide market-specific amenity recommendations based on advanced data analytics, and we treat your property with the same care and attention we would give our own. Our mission isn't just management‚Äîit's maximizing your property's potential in ways others simply can't match.

How much does full service management cost?

Our pricing structure for full-service management is based on a percentage of the total revenue generated by your property, minus platform management fees and taxes. On average, our management fee ranges from 12% to 18% of the total revenue.

What sets your amenity recommendations apart?

Our amenity recommendations aren't based on guesswork‚Äîthey're derived from advanced market analysis software and years of experience in your specific location. We analyze thousands of bookings to determine exactly which amenities will deliver the greatest ROI for your property, whether it's a hot tub, premium coffee setup, or specialized kitchen equipment.

How does your in-person property assessment work?

We personally visit each property we manage to conduct a comprehensive assessment. During this visit, we evaluate the property's condition, identify potential improvements, test all systems and amenities, and take detailed notes to ensure our team can operate everything efficiently. This hands-on approach allows us to spot opportunities and issues that would be missed in a standard virtual onboarding process.

Who pays for the handyman, cleaners, and other service providers?

As the property owner, you are responsible for covering the costs associated with handyman services, cleaners, and other service providers. These costs are not included in our management fee but rather are reimbursed directly through a saved payment method on file.

Can I choose my own service providers, or do you have preferred vendors?

We work with a network of trusted local service providers, including cleaners, handymen, and maintenance professionals. While we have preferred vendors for reliability and quality assurance, we are open to coordinating with your preferred service providers upon request.

What exactly does short-term rental management entail?

Short-term rental management involves handling various aspects of renting out your property on a short-term basis, such as marketing, guest communication, pricing optimization, property maintenance, and guest services.

How do you ensure my property is well taken care of during guest stays?

We have a rigorous screening process for guests, ensuring they meet our criteria and are respectful of your property. Additionally, we provide 24/7 guest support and work with trusted local service providers for cleaning and maintenance. Our hands-on approach means we're familiar with your property's specific needs and can address issues quickly.

Can I still use my property for personal use?

Absolutely! You have the flexibility to block off dates for personal use or for friends and family. Our goal is to maximize your property's income while accommodating your needs.

How do you handle pricing for my property?

We utilize dynamic pricing tools and market analysis to adjust rates based on factors like demand, seasonality, and local events. Our goal is to optimize your income while remaining competitive in the market. Our sophisticated software analyzes thousands of data points specific to your market to ensure your property is always priced optimally.
