Frequently asked questions

How much does full service management cost?

Our pricing structure for full-service management is based on a percentage of the total revenue generated by your property, minus platform management fees and taxes. On average, our management fee ranges from 12% to 18% of the total revenue. 

Who pays for the handyman, cleaners, and other service providers?

As the property owner, you are responsible for covering the costs associated with handyman services, cleaners, and other service providers. These costs are not included in our management fee but rather are reimbursed directly to Divergent through a saved payment method on file.

Can I choose my own service providers, or do you have preferred vendors?

We work with a network of trusted local service providers, including cleaners, handymen, and maintenance professionals. While we have preferred vendors for reliability and quality assurance, we are open to coordinating with your preferred service providers upon request.

What exactly does short-term rental management entail?

Short-term rental management involves handling various aspects of renting out your property on a short-term basis, such as marketing, guest communication, pricing optimization, property maintenance, and guest services.

How do you ensure my property is well taken care of during guest stays?

We have a rigorous screening process for guests, ensuring they meet our criteria and are respectful of your property. Additionally, we provide 24/7 guest support and work with trusted local service providers for cleaning and maintenance.

Can I still use my property for personal use?

Absolutely! You have the flexibility to block off dates for personal use or for friends and family. Our goal is to maximize your property’s income while accommodating your needs.

How do you handle pricing for my property?

We utilize dynamic pricing tools and market analysis to adjust rates based on factors like demand, seasonality, and local events. Our goal is to optimize your income while remaining competitive in the market.

What kind of marketing strategies do you employ to attract guests?

We use a combination of online marketing channels, including listing your property on popular rental platforms, optimizing your listing for search visibility, and leveraging social media and email marketing to reach potential guests.

What is your process for handling guest communication?

Our team of dedicated guest-facing agents is available 24/7 to respond to guest inquiries, provide support throughout their stay, and address any issues promptly and professionally.

How do you ensure the safety and security of my property?

We conduct thorough background checks on guests, implement strict house rules, and install security measures like keyless entry systems. Additionally, we have protocols in place to handle emergencies and security incidents.

What kind of reporting and analytics do you provide?

We provide detailed reports on your property’s performance, including occupancy rates, revenue generated, guest reviews, and maintenance activities. This helps you track the success of your investment and make informed decisions.

What happens if there are damages to my property during a guest's stay?

We have insurance policies in place to cover damages caused by guests. Additionally, we conduct thorough inspections before and after each stay to document the condition of the property and address any issues promptly.
