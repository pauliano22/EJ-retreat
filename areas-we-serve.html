Frequently Asked Questions

What cities does E&J Retreats manage Airbnb properties in? +
We manage short-term rental properties in 80+ markets across the United States — including Florida, Tennessee, Texas, Arizona, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Colorado, Utah, Nevada, California, and more. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific market.
Does E&J Retreats manage properties outside of Florida? +
Yes. While we're headquartered in the Tampa Bay area, we operate as a national STR management company. We serve major markets including Nashville, Gatlinburg, Scottsdale, Austin, Dallas, Asheville, Myrtle Beach, and many more cities across the country.
How do you manage properties in cities where you don't have a local office? +
We operate through a vetted local vendor network in each market — local cleaning crews, maintenance teams, and inspectors held to our standards. Our centralized team handles guest communication, dynamic pricing, and revenue management from headquarters. National-level expertise with local execution.
What is your management fee for short-term rental properties? +
We charge a percentage of gross rental revenue with no hidden fees. The exact rate varies by market and property type. Get a free revenue report and we'll include our full fee structure alongside your property's projected earnings — so you can see the net numbers before making any decision.
My city isn't on the list — can you still manage my property? +
Possibly. We're continuously expanding into new markets. If your city isn't listed, contact us and we'll let you know if we can accommodate your property or when we plan to expand to your area. We onboard new markets regularly.
What does "full-service" STR management actually include? +
Full-service means we handle everything: listing creation and optimization across 15+ platforms, dynamic pricing, guest screening and communication, housekeeping coordination, maintenance oversight, owner reporting, and compliance with local STR permit requirements. You collect your revenue — we do the rest.
